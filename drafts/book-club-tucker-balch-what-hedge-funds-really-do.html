What Hedge Funds Really Do: An Introduction to Portfolio Management

How they make money from the market?

As the name Hedge Fund suggested, hedge funds usually do long/short 'hedge' trading strategies to make money. For people who don't faimilar with the terms, long means buy the stock and sell when the price goes higher, and short means buy (not exactly) the stock and make money when the price is lower. An example would be long the 10 best performing stocks and short the 10 worst performing stocks if you can.

Besides this, another strategy is arbitrage.
